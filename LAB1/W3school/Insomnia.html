What is Insomnia?

Insomnia is a common sleep disorder that makes it hard to fall asleep, stay asleep, or get restful sleep. Here are some quick facts:

  • Difficulty falling asleep: Lying awake for hours.
  • Frequent waking: Waking up multiple times during the night.
  • Feeling tired: Even after sleeping, you feel unrefreshed.
  • Impact: Insomnia can affect your mood, health, and productivity.
